THE TRANSFER[XFR] STATION

THE TRANSFER STATION is a living productive community for collaborators, innovators, and entrepreneurs - housed in a facility designed to test your ideas, find new inspirations, and grow your business through the support of like-minded individuals. Our Manayunk-based industrial space has been re-imagined and re-invented into a modern nest where you can comfortably develop and share your work, products, creations, and community endeavors. By offering both physical and virtual resources tailored to suit your specific needs, we provide the best of the collaborative worlds, the business incubators, the creative labs, and the retail marketplaces - a home in which the culture of support and creation creates accelerated serendipity.

THE LAYOUT

Our facility has been designed with adaptive reuse at its core - completely retrofitting an abandoned industrial building in the heart of Philadelphia's Manayunk neighborhood to accommodate a mix of working, making, learning, selling, and collaborating. Our spaces are designed to be as comfortable as they are impactful, creating a warm and friendly facility in which you can make your best first impression.
